Summary: City Latitude V. Humidity

The large data clusters in the high humidity range confirm that we are living in a big, wet world. However, the greater number of clusters in the high end of N latitude are difficult to compare to their southern counterparts. There are more habitable land masses in the northern latitude than the southern. Christchurch, NZ, is the furthermost southern capital at approximately 43 degrees south. As a comparison, Athens, the southernmost capital of Europe, is between 37 and 38 degrees south. Moscow, the largest city in Europe, is at 56 degrees north.
